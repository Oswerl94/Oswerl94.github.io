Why Tire Pressure Matters

Proper tire pressure keeps your motorcycle safe, improves fuel efficiency, and extends tire life. Riding with low or high pressure can lead to poor handling, uneven wear, or even tire blowouts.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Make sure your tires are cool before checking the pressure.

2. Find the recommended tire pressure in your bikeâ€™s manual or on the tire sidewall.

3. Remove the valve cap and use a tire pressure gauge to get a reading.

4. Adjust the pressure using a pump or release air if overinflated.

5. Replace the valve cap once done.

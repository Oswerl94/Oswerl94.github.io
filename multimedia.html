Why Tire Pressure Matters

Proper tire pressure keeps your motorcycle safe, improves fuel efficiency, and extends tire life. Riding with low or high pressure can lead to poor handling, uneven wear, or even tire blowouts.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Make sure your tires are cool before checking the pressure.

2. Find the recommended tire pressure in your bike’s owner’s manual or on the tire sidewall.

3. Remove the valve cap from your tire’s air valve.

4. Press a tire pressure gauge firmly onto the valve stem and read the pressure.

5. If it’s too low, add air using a pump. If it’s too high, release some air carefully.

6. Replace the valve cap when done.
